About me

Hi there! I’m a dedicated software engineer with a degree in Computer Science and a minor in Business Administration from the American University in Cairo. Growing up in the dynamic environment of the UAE, I recently relocated to the US, driven by my passion for new challenges and opportunities.

With three years of experience in software engineering, I’ve built a solid foundation in Agile methodologies and cross-functional collaboration. I’ve crafted user stories, made accurate estimates, and effectively managed tasks using tools like JIRA. My technical background empowers me to connect with developers while ensuring the user’s voice is always at the forefront.

What truly excites me about product management is the chance to dive deeper into user needs and pain points. I often find myself jotting down product improvements and innovative ideas in my notes—an instinct that reflects my commitment to creating solutions that resonate with users. I’m eager to leverage my engineering expertise and empathetic approach to drive impactful product development.

Having lived in multiple countries and being fluent in both English and Arabic, with a current focus on learning Spanish, I thrive in diverse environments. This multicultural background equips me with the ability to work effectively with people from various backgrounds and perspectives.

In my free time, I love to gather hobbies! Whether it’s kayaking, hiking, riding beach buggies, or traveling, I’m always seeking new adventures. I also enjoy crafting, with skills in basic sewing and crochet, and I have a passion for horseback riding.
